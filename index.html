Juicy Chicken

This will be you're favorite recipe! Super simple. All made inside a croc pot.

Ingredients :

  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tsp Garlic
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tsp Pepper
  • 1 Tsp Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare Chicken and place inside croc pot
  2. Combine all above ingredients into a small bowl of your choice
  3. Add everything to croc pot and mix
  4. Cover and cook on LOW for 4-6 hours
  5. Shred chicken
  6. Let sit in sauce for 15 minutes and serve!
